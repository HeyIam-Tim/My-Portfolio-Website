Ribbon Firework

An ecommerce website for a local shop that sells hand-made items made out of colorful ribbons. Here visitors and users can view and explore product, put items into a cart, and then order any product they would like.

Solved Problems:

• increased sales by 30%(20 -> 26 clients/month).
• easy for users to view, order and track items.
• easy for owners to manage customers' and product data.

Used Technologies:

Django, Django REST Framework, Postgres, CSS/SASS, HTML, Vanilla JavaScript, S3 bucket, Heroku.
