Instagram Clone

An Instagram like social media platform with main functionalities such as register and login; view, like and comment posts; follow and view profiles; upload, update and delete images; switch a color theme, reset the password and more. Here people can interact with their and their friends' content as they would do on typical social media.

Used Technologies:

Django, Django REST Framework, Postgres, AWS(RDS/S3), Heroku, HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS.
