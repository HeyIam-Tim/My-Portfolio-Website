First Hair Salon

A website for a local hair salon nearby me. This is a really great place and I love it. Yet they had nothing but a social media group at that time, which was so inconvenient for people. With that said I decided to help them and make them a website.

Solved Problems:

• increased sales by 15%(90 -> 114 clients/month).
• visitors can easily view services in one place.
• owners can manage product data.
• visitors no longer need to use a social media group.

Used Technologies:

Django, Django REST Framework, Postgres, SASS/CSS, HTML, Vanilla JavaScript, Cloudinary, Heroku.
